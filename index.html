Games by Daniel Acevedo

The Dreamer

November 2024

Link: https://website.com

Description:
This game is for a group project and is still in early development. It is supposed to be a dreamlike blend of reality and fiction, where the player is a servicemember who is struggling with PTSD but is experiencing surreal phenomena, like explosions of teddy bears. The game is being made in Unity 3D.

Turtle Tank Defense

October 2024

Link: https://dacevedo11.itch.io/turtle-tank-defense

Description:
This is a tower defense game where you place Turtle Tanks to destroy plastic waste before it can reach the ocean. Waves of trash spawn each round, and the amount of trash increases with each round. My motivation for making this game was to educate players about the effects of littering and plastic pollution. Made in Unity 3D.
